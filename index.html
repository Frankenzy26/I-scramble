How to Play

  • Classic: Relaxed play. No timer, infinite lives.
  • Blitz: 60 seconds total. Correct answers add 5 seconds.
  • Hardcore: 1 Life. 15 seconds PER WORD. No hints.
  • Survival: Build word streaks! 3 lives, no hints, streak multiplier.
  • Arcade: Combo system! Keep answering to build combos for bonus points.
  • Custom: You set the rules.

Tips:

  • Press Enter to submit answer
  • Use Skip if you're stuck (costs 1 life in modes with lives)
  • Longer words give more points
  • Save your word lists in the Bank for later
  • Use Quick Play to start with pre-made categories

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Enter: Submit answer
  • Escape: Pause game
  • Ctrl+H: Get hint
  • Ctrl+G: Give up

Use the Bank to save your lists or export them to share with friends!
